Cost Optimization

AcmeCorp's Data Agent costs have ballooned to $45,000/month — three times the approved budget. The CFO has given you 30 days to cut costs significantly without degrading agent quality. Navigate 5 optimization decisions.

Mission Brief
Current spend: $45K/month across 200 stores, ~50,000 queries/month. Target: reduce to under $15K/month (67% reduction) while maintaining 85%+ user satisfaction. You have 5 levers to pull — model selection, caching, context compression, routing, and financial guardrails. Aim for 5/5.
