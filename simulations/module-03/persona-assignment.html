Assign the Right Persona

AcmeCorp faces 5 real-world situations across their 200-store retail operation. Your job: deploy the right persona (agent type) for each crisis, request, or project.

Mission Brief
Each scenario presents a situation and three candidate personas. Pick the right one — correct assignments earn a point, wrong ones show you why the mismatch causes problems. Aim for 5/5.
