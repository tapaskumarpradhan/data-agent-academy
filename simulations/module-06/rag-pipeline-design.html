RAG Pipeline Design

AcmeCorp wants its Data Agent to answer questions using internal business documents — policy manuals, product guides, and regional reports. You must design the RAG pipeline from ingestion through generation.

Mission Brief
The agent must answer questions like "What is our return policy for electronics?" and "Which region had the highest margin last quarter?" across 500+ internal documents. Make 5 design decisions about chunking, retrieval, context budgeting, and query planning — aim for 5/5.
