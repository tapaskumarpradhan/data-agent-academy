Your Mission Brief
LIVE CASE STUDY
▸ SITUATION

AcmeCorp is a mid-size retail company with 200 stores nationwide. Their data team is drowning — analysts spend 70% of their time writing SQL for stakeholders instead of doing actual analysis.

▸ OBJECTIVE

Deploy a self-service Data Agent — so any stakeholder can ask questions in plain English and get accurate, governed answers from the company's data warehouse. No SQL required.

▸ YOUR ROLE

You've been hired to design, build, and operate it. This training follows AcmeCorp end-to-end — every architecture decision, guardrail, and trade-off is grounded in their real constraints.
